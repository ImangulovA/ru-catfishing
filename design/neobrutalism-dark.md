## Overview

Dark counterpart to neobrutalism: the same bold borders and vivid accent colors, moved onto deep, high-contrast dark surfaces with hard offset shadows.

## Style Foundations

- **Visual style:** modern, clean, high-contrast, dark
- **Typography scale:** 13/15/17/21/27/35
- **Typography fonts:** primary=Inter, display=Inter, mono=JetBrains Mono
- **Typography weights:** 100, 200, 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900
- **Color palette:** primary, neutral, success, warning, danger
- **Spacing scale:** 4/8/12/16/24/32
- **Elevation:** hard offset shadows; shadow ink stays darker than the surface, borders stay lighter than the card.

## Colors

- **Primary (#FDC800):** Signature yellow, reused from the light system so the brand pops on dark.
- **Secondary (#8B72FF):** Brightened purple for legibility on dark surfaces.
- **Success (#22C55E):** Brightened success token for dark contrast.
- **Warning (#F59E0B):** Brightened warning token for dark contrast.
- **Danger (#F4365A):** Brightened danger token for dark contrast.
- **Surface (#14181F):** Deep neutral page surface.
- **Text (#F4F5F7):** Near-white body text for AA contrast on surface.
- **Neutral (#14181F):** Derived from the surface token for official format compatibility.
